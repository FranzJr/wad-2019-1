Course goal

The purpose of this course is involve the students in all the life cycle of web applications development and give some analytics tools to the students, in order their has own criteria to take decisions on a software project in the real world with real needs and faced the client requirements with innovation, creativity and knowledge. This web applications will be satisface the particularly needs of clients and that needs will defined the quality attributes and functional requirements.
